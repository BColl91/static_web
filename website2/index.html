This is the second website

My teenage hobby and degree in Costume Making reminds me of some fun times where I learned decent life-skills along with boosting creativity and how to work with different strange and wonderful materials.


I got to make Disney's Zorro, a rendition of Red Riding Hood, multiple periodic dresses, a *lot* of cloaks, some animal accessories, a full armor suit, and more! It was really fun helping build characters and making them come to life with the actors and models.


You might be wondering what happened.. It is very difficult to get in the industry in both theatre and television. So I slowly lost interest as I worked other jobs. Along the way I was given a chance to learn more digital-based skills and a new perspective that showed I can work with another interest of mine: computers!
